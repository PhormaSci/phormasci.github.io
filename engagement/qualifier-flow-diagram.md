## Data Collected Per Path

### All Paths Collect:
- Organization type (Q1)
- Main challenge (Q2)
- Preferred next step (Q3)

### Additional Data by Path:

**Survey Path:**
- Survey type preference (Q4: Academic vs Industry)
- Link opened confirmation (Q5 or Q6)

**Call Booking Path:**
- Email address (Q7)

**Resources Path:**
- Email address (Q8)
- Topic interests (Q9)

**Newsletter Path:**
- Email address (Q10, optional)
