A comprehensive deep dive into Deep Learning for microscopy imaging. Learn to train, validate, and deploy custom segmentation models using modern architectures. Emphasis on ground truth quality and avoiding overconfident predictions.

- Ground truth annotation strategies & quality control
- Model architecture selection (StarDist, Cellpose, U-Net)
- Cloud-based training pipelines (GPU optimization)
- Validation metrics & error analysis
- Production inference deployment & monitoring
